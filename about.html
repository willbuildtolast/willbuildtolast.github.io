About me

I am a college student studying Psychology at New York Univeristy based on Manhattan. Currently I am focusing on neuropsychology and I am interested in exploring the neural mechanisms behind our behaviors and actions.

I was born in China and I came to America during my high school years. I embrace the rich cultural diversity and the harmonious coexistence of different cultures.

My passion lies in the interdisciplinary realm of psychology and neuroscience, crossing the boundaries between psychology, biology, and engineering. Meanwhile, I have aspired to improve the world through innovations. I am deeply passionate about translating ideas and knowledge of neuroscience into tangible solutions that can significantly improve our overall well-being.
